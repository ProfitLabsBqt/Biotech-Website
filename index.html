The Distillation Journey

How we refine raw grains into ultra-pure ethanol.

Step 1: Raw Material Sourcing & Milling

We strictly source damaged grains (damaged rice and maize) through the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and other verified ethical supply chains. Grains are milled into a fine meal to expose starches.

Step 2: Liquefaction & Cooker Stage

The milled flour is mixed with water and liquefying enzymes. Cooked under steam, the starch is converted into fermentable dextrins and simple sugars.

Step 3: Continuous Fermentation

The saccharified mash is cooled and transferred to fermentation tanks. Under temperature-controlled conditions, high-yield yeast strains convert sugars into alcohol over a 50-hour cycle.

Step 4: Multi-Stage Distillation & Dehydration

Using advanced multi-column distillation towers, the alcohol is separated and purified. A final molecular sieve dehydration stage removes remaining moisture, yielding >99.9% purity.

Step 5: Rigorous In-House Quality Control

Our laboratory performs GC-MS and detailed chemical parameter analyses on every batch. Approved material is then loaded directly into custom tanker trucks for transport.

Development Roadmap

Our journey of scaling high-capacity distillery operations.

Project Conception

Civil & Utility Construction

Groundbreaking and civil works in Bemetara, Chhattisgarh. Installed state-of-the-art fermentation, distillation, and molecular sieve structures.
